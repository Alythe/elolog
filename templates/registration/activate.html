Invalid activation key

Invalid activation key, this can have one of the following reasons:
  • Your account already is activated. If you can't login, try resetting your password.
  • The key has expired. In this case, try resending the activation mail.
  • If nothing of the above applies, try contacting an admin (see Contact).
