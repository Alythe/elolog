About Elolog

Elolog is a place for you to keep track of your League of Legends game experiences. This helps you seeing your improvements faster and start working on whatever deficits you have.

  • You can view your log entries in a graph, to see how your Elo changed since you started.
  • Elolog does not try to lock your data up, if you decide to stop using it you can export everything you entered as CSV formatted files.
  • Registration is free and painless
  • Your logs are private, no one can see them.
  • If you want to share your logs you can publish them under a safe URL. Doing this enables all visitors (even non-registered) to view your log when you share the URL. This can be useful if you give lessons and want to watch the progress of your disciples.
  • You can create as many logs as you want (e.g. for different accounts)
